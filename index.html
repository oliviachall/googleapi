NORTH CAROLINA


No matter where you live in North Carolina, you are never far from the natural beauty that defines this state. From picnicking among the statuesque mountains in the Blue Ridge Parkway, to canoeing on a lake in Raleigh, to exploring historic military forts overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, North Carolina State Parks offer access to this natural beauty. With over 40 state parks scattered across the state, there are plenty of opportunities to get out there and experience the natural beauty of North Carolina.
